CORRECTED TALOS HVAC SCORING RUBRIC - SERVICE TECHNICIAN

Rules:
- Experience is the most important factor
- Required experience: candidate has exact or more experience than required amount
- Close to required experience: candidate has 50-95% of required experience
- Not close to required experience: candidate has under 50% of required experience
- Good resume: proper formatting, no typos, proper punctuation/grammar, at least 2 substantive bullets per experience
- Mid resume: professional formatting with few grammatical errors, but missing key elements like bullets under experiences, or has limited bullets, or has small formatting issues
- Poor resume: multiple punctuation errors, multiple typos, numerous formatting issues, AND lacks substantive content
- No work gap: under 6 months of unemployment
- Small work gap: 6 months to 1 year of unemployment
- Large work gap: over 1 year of unemployment
- Job hoppy: frequent job changes (significant negative factor)
- Distance: <30 miles (minimal impact), 30-50 miles (moderate impact), >50 miles (major impact)
- Certifications listed always scores higher than certifications not listed
- OVERQUALIFIED CLAUSE: Applies when candidate's previous job title is significantly senior to the position being applied for, resulting in score of 70-75 (low end of "give them a chance" tier). This is based on job titles, not years of experience.

OVERQUALIFIED EXAMPLES:
  HVAC-specific roles applying down:
  - HVAC General Manager → Entry-level Dispatcher/CSR: OVERQUALIFIED
  - HVAC Operations Director → Dispatcher/Administrative roles: OVERQUALIFIED
  - Lead HVAC Technician → Maintenance Technician: OVERQUALIFIED
  - HVAC Service Manager → HVAC Technician: OVERQUALIFIED
  - Senior HVAC Technician → Helper/Apprentice: OVERQUALIFIED

  Non-HVAC roles applying to entry-level positions:
  - Director of Marketing → Administrative Assistant: OVERQUALIFIED
  - Director of Operations → Customer Service Representative: OVERQUALIFIED
  - Senior Manager (any field) → Entry-level Clerical: OVERQUALIFIED
  - Vice President → Receptionist/Dispatcher: OVERQUALIFIED
  - Department Head → Administrative Support: OVERQUALIFIED

  IMPORTANT: Overqualification is about title seniority mismatch, NOT experience years. A technician with 10+ years applying to a technician role is NOT overqualified.
